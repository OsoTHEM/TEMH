Police often protect the people, bringing them safety, and being there to bring the community together. Yet, there has been major incidents with police brutality, police have killed over 100 black people who were unarmed in mid-2014. Out of the races, black people have been the most killed by police officers. In the years police officers have claimed it was in self defense and blame it on the victims

Here are a few stories of victims with their cases. Be aware that these are a short description of the cases, but does have a big impact.

St. Anthony police Officer Jeronimo Yanez pulled Castile over during a traffic stop. Castile told the officer he had a firearm on him, which he was permitted to carry. Castile reached for something and Yanez shot him five times. During the Facebook Live video, Castile's girlfriend said he was reaching for his license; the officer later said he thought Castile was reaching for his gun.

Terence Crutcher was a victim of a police brutality. He was killed by police officer Betty Jo. At the time he was unarmed and was only standing by his car.
