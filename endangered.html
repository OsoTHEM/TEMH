Polar Bear

Polar bears are beautiful white creatures that live in the arctic circle. They have white fur to help them blend in the snow to catch their prey. These bears have always been the top animal to see, but yet there are few polar bears around the world.

The bears have been slowly vanishing because of global warming. Polar bears have lived on ice and snow, and because of global warming its been melting fast. The bears have been starving and had often drowned.

Hawaiian monk seal

Hawaiian monk seal is a adorable seal that swims far from where humans are. They live in Hawaii, but in recent studies show that these animals have been dropping population. Hawaiian monk seal would eat fish, however fishermen have been overfishing around the area. This was causing the seals to starve and die off.

African Elephant are almost like humans. The elephant females would protect and care for their young. The family would always stick together, and if something happens the family would be there to help each other. However, these amazing creatures have been dying. Animal sellers would take the elephants and sell them off. Another shocking fact is that hunters would kill these animals and would remove their tusks. They would use the tusks for fashion and other things. Its a heart break to see amazing animals with feeling like humans to be killed in a horrible way.
